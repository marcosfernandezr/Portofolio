SURA: Independent workers

UX Research

Semester project of the course Anthro-Design. The objective of this course was to establish three design opportunities for a challenge proposed by a counterpart. The three statements have to emerge from research and the analysis of the data gathered. In greater detail, the course consisted of many field trips, where it was possible to interview more than 25 people with different jobs, for example, entrepreneurs, freelancers, consultants, among others.

View more

For my team, the counterpart was a company from the Financial Services and Capital Markets sector. The challenge was to build relationships with independent workers to offer them suited products. Also, to know why some Chilean independent workers don't have savings or investments.

What I did

  • UX Research: Interviews, Personas and data analysis
  • Graphic design: Report and graphic content
