DigiKit

Product + App design

Due to COVID-19, children in cancer rehabilitation had to attend the sessions via Zoom. The remote format brought many problems, for example, not having enough control over the session and the lack of field of view in videocalls. Hence, the creation of DigiKit as a solution for the context above.


DigiKit is a set of two products: a board and a tablet app that help occupational therapists to evaluate graphomotricity in remote rehabilitation sessions. In more detail, the definition of graphomotricity refers to all of the movements that kids need to learn to write.

What I did

  • UX Research: Interviews, Personas, Journey Maps
  • Product Design: 3D Modeling,rendering and animation
  • App design: Graphic design attributes
